Tortillas

  • Jump to Recipe

Fresh, warm, and flavorful tortillas

Tortillas are much better when they are fresh.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1/3 cup canola oil
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. Add the flour to a bowl
  2. Pour oil over the flour
  3. In a seperate glass, disolve your salt in warm water
  4. Pour salted water into the bowl with the flour and oil
  5. Knead for 10 minutes
  6. Break the dough into 16 balls
  7. Allow to rise for 30 minutes
  8. Roll each ball of dough as flat as you can
    • It does not need to be a perfect circle
  9. Heat a pan on medium heat
  10. Cook the tortillas for 30 seconds on each side
  11. Enjoy
