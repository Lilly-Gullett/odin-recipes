Boozy Milkshakes

A delicious butter pecan milkshake with alcohol

This recipe is great for summer evenings outside.

The measurements below serve 2, adjust as needed.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup vanilla ice cream
  • 1/3 cup of milk
  • 1/3 cup Ole Smokey Butter Pecan Moonshines

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients in the blender
  2. Blend well
  3. Pour into 2 glasses
  4. Enjoy
