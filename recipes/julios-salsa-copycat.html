Julio's Salsa Copycat Recipe

The most smooth, flavorful salsa at home

This salsa was a heavy part of my childhood in Texas. This salsa was enjoyed by my family lake-side, pool-side, and on the table at every Sunday Dinner.

In 2021, I moved to Colorado. I was heartbroken to discover that I could not find the refrigerated Julio's salsa at any of my new local grocery stores. So I started trying to recreate it. This is the best copycat I have found.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of diced tomatoes (14.5 oz)
  • 1/2 cup of tomato juice
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of citric acid
  • 1 teaspoon of minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pickle crisp
  • 1/2 teaspoon MSG
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cumin
  • 4 jalepe√±os

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to the blender
  2. Blend well
  3. Pour into a lidded container and place in fridge
  4. Allow the flavors to mingle for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight
  5. Enjoy with tortilla chips
